Pineapple Sunrise Mimosas

Description

Pineapple Sunrise Mimosas are a delightful twist on the classic mimosa, combining vibrant citrus flavors with the effervescence of sparkling wine.

Ingredients

  • 4 ½ cups pineapple juice
  • 1 ⅛ cups orange juice
  • ⅓ cup lime juice
  • ¼ fresh pineapple, sliced
  • ½ blood orange, sliced
  • ½ lime, sliced
  • 1 (750 milliliter) bottle Prosecco (Italian sparkling wine)
  • ¼ cup Campari
  • 4 slices pineapple and orange slices, for garnish

Steps

  1. Stir pineapple juice, orange juice, and lime juice together in a large pitcher or punch bowl. Add pineapple, blood orange, and lime slices. Cover and refrigerate for 2 to 24 hours.
  2. To serve, pour 1/2 cup juice mixture into each glass or champagne flute. Add 1/4 cup Prosecco per serving. Slowly drizzle in 1 teaspoon Campari. Garnish each mimosa with a fresh pineapple or orange slice.

    3. Tips

    For a non-alcoholic version, substitute club soda or citrus-flavored sparkling water for the Prosecco, and use grenadine instead of Campari.
